Recipe #1 - Burger with French Fries

  • Time: 15min
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Meat 1 slice
Bread buns 2
Cheese 2 slices
Potatoes 2 grams

Step 1: Place the meat, and cheese between the two bread buns.

Step 2: Fry the potatoes for 5 min.

Step 3: Put any sauce you want and serve.

Recipe #2 - Chocolate Sweets
